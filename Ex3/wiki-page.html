Vietnamese Cuisine

Vietnamese cuisine refers to the cooking traditions and practices of Vietnam. It is known for its balance of flavors such as sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.

Fresh ingredients and herbs play an important role in Vietnamese dishes, making the food both healthy and flavorful.

Vietnamese cuisine has developed over centuries and has been influenced by geography, climate, and cultural exchange.
